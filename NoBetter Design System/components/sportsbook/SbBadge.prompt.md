Dark-theme chip for status, live indicators, and — critically — NoBetter's editorial message inside a real-looking book.

```jsx
<SbBadge variant="live" live>En vivo 67'</SbBadge>
<SbBadge variant="accent">+142 mercados</SbBadge>
<SbBadge variant="truth">Margen casa 6.8%</SbBadge>
<SbBadge variant="ficticia">Saldo ficticio</SbBadge>
<SbBadge variant="cash">Cobrado</SbBadge>
```

**`truth` / `ficticia`** (amber) are the design's conscience: they sit in the same visual register as a real book's promo chips, but state the house edge and the fictional nature of the money plainly. Keep them present, not hidden.
**`live`** adds a pulsing red dot — only for genuinely live events.
